嘉兴市南溪中学-1968-South

嘉兴市南溪中学创立于1968年，历经普明寺中学、利明中学、东栅中心校、东栅乡中学等的变革，于2008年原地新建，地处嘉兴市南湖新区中心，占地55亩。学校拥 有300米环形塑胶跑道的运动场、室内体育馆、篮球场、排球场、 乒乓室、语音室、电脑教室、电子备课室、多媒体教室、校园音响系统、闭路电视和计算机网络系统等现代化教育设施。

Jiaxing Nanxi Middle School was founded in 1968, after the transformation of Puming Temple Middle School, Liming Middle School, Dongzha Central School, Dongzha Township Middle School, etc., it was newly built in 2008, located in the center of Nanhu New District, Jiaxing City, covering an area of 55 acres. The school has a 300-meter circular plastic track sports field, indoor gymnasium, basketball court, volleyball court, table tennis room, speech room, computer classroom, electronic preparation room, multimedia classroom, campus sound system, closed-circuit television and computer network system and other modern educational facilities.
